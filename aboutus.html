About Us

Extraordinary Family Int'l Church was birthed on the 25th of Dec 2016. The Vision was impressed in the heart of the Lead Pastor in 2009 after a time of personal prayers where the Holy Spirit said these words "Build with me a great house that will provide spiritual cover for all who agree to operate by covenant principles for the earth will burn like an oven but as you build this house with one voice having one language, nothing shall be restrained from you which you imagine to do. the earth shall be subdued before you in DOMINION". From then till 2016 the Lord led the lead pastor to be trained in ministry affairs for seven years in LFC, Winners Chapel where he worked as a volunteer office staff without a salary.   The Church finally got started that blessed Christmas day at Forever Friends, 1st Avenue, Festac Town, Lagos with just the lead pastor in attendance.Our service time was 6:30a.m as that was the only available time at the facility, but then the Church grew to eight members within the first month i.e six adults and two Children. We are presently at our 4th location where God had lead us within the space of 5 years. Within this period, it has been a glorious journey with God with visible increase and testimonies in the lives of attenders. As I write we just celebrated our 5th year Church anniversary and this year alone we've had two sets of Pastors/Ministers ordaination in Extraordinary Family Church. The first ordination was held in February 2021, 2 Pastors and 4 Ministers were ordained while the second one, was held in November 2021, 1 Pastor and 2 ministers were ordained. Our Sunday service is held at 9am, with leadership masterclass starts at 8am. We also hold Wednesday mid-week service at 6.30pm

Meet Our Pastors

Ifeanyi David Emeaghara

Lead Pastor

Ifeanyi D. Emeaghara

Lead Pastor

 

Born on the 16th of March 1982, Ifeanyi David Emeaghara rededicated his life to Christ in 2005 under the ministry of Bishop David Oyedepo and straight away became fully involved in the affairs of the kingdom. Having served faithfully in Living Faith Church (LFC) aka Winners Chapel for a decade, he received the call from God to start Extraordinary Family Int'l Church which commenced on the 25th of December 2016 in Lagos, Nigeria. Pastor Ifeanyi was fully ordained and commissioned into full time pastoral ministry by Bishop David Oyedepo on the 25th of February 2017. He attended his primary/secondary education in Lagos and university education in Ogun State University now Olabisi Onabanjo University (OOU) where he studied Computer and Data Processing. He is married to Pastor Maryrose Ifeoma Emeaghara and they are blessed with three boys, David, Isreal & Michael.
