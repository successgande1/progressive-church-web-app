Our Core Values

People

People are the greatest assets on earth,the human capital is the most important factor of production. The worth of the human soul is of inestimable value Jesus...Christ cane all the way from heaven to give his life for the redemption of people from destruction. He said in Matt16:26 what shall it profit a man if he gains the whole world and lose his soul. At Extraordinary Family we believe that the Church is all about the welfare of people spirit, soul and body(1st thess 5:23) and we make that our priority.

Purpose

It is said that a man that does not know why he was born is not fit to live.At Extraordinary we believe we're all created to be an extension of God's kingdom all over the earth through ...the discovery and deployment of our S.H.A.P..E(Skills Heart Abilities Passion Experiences). We strive to help people find their SHAPE so they can live fulfilled and enjoyable lives.

Progress

The life of the justified or saved individual is Meant to shine brighter and get better consistently until the perfect day which means the appearing of Jesus (Prov 4:18)...Here at Extraordinary we believe that as you serve God genuinely you've secured for yourself a life of endless advancement on all fronts(1st Sam 12:6) and so we create innumerable platforms that afford everyone that golden opportunity for kingdom stewardship where your gifts and skills can find profitable expression.

Purity

At Extraordinary Family we believe our final home and destination is with God in heaven,we call it God's retirement plan for all His children by virtue of our sojourn and stewardship to... Him on earth.However, eternity at last with God is impossible without a life of purity.Hebrews 12:13-14 says without holiness no man can meet with God.The journey to purity begins by surrendering the totality of your life to Christ.If you haven't done that already pls do today and join us on this amazing journey.
